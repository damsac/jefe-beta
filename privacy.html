Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

The short version: Your audio never leaves your phone — speech is turned into text on your device. To build your paperwork, only the text is sent to our AI provider. We don't sell your data, and we don't use your voice in the cloud.

What Jefe does with your voice

When you record a walk, the speech-to-text happens on your device. Your audio recordings are not uploaded to us or to anyone else. To turn your words into an estimate, invoice, or report, the resulting text transcript is sent to a third-party AI processing provider that generates the document and returns it to your device. That text is used only to produce your output; it is not sold, and we do not use it to build advertising profiles.

What we collect

  • On your device (not sent to us): your business details (name, city, license number), your walks, transcripts, photos you take, and the documents you create. These live on your device and are included in documents you choose to share.
  • Transcript text: sent to our AI provider only to generate your documents, as described above.
  • Waitlist: if you join the waitlist on our website, we collect the email address (and optional trade) you submit, through our form provider (Formspree), so we can tell you about the app.
  • Beta diagnostics: because Jefe is distributed through Apple TestFlight, Apple may share crash logs, basic usage data, and any feedback you send with us, under Apple's privacy terms.

What we don't do

  • We don't upload or store your audio recordings.
  • We don't sell your personal information.
  • We don't show you ads or build advertising profiles.

How long we keep it

On-device data stays on your device until you delete it or remove the app. Waitlist emails are kept until you ask us to remove them or the beta ends. Transcript text sent for processing is not retained by us beyond generating your document.

Your choices

  • You can turn off microphone access anytime in iOS Settings (Jefe won't be able to hear your walk).
  • You can delete a walk or document in the app, or remove the app to clear on-device data.
  • You can ask us to delete your waitlist email by emailing us.

Children

Jefe is a tool for working professionals and is not intended for anyone under 18.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update the date above. Material changes will be reflected here.
