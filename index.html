How it works

Three steps. No typing.

If you can talk through a job, you can use Jefe. Nothing to learn.

01

Walk & talk

Tap once, then just talk. Jefe listens the whole time and writes down what matters.

02

Fix anything

It's a first draft. Tap any line to fix a word or add a price. You're always in control.

03

One tap → paperwork

Turn it into an estimate, invoice, or report — with your logo on it. Then text or email it.

Get it on your phone

4 steps, about 2 minutes.

Jefe isn't on the App Store yet — you'll get it through TestFlight, Apple's official free app for trying apps early. Open this page on your iPhone and follow along.

1

Install TestFlight

TestFlight is a free Apple app for trying apps before they hit the App Store. Get it once — you'll open Jefe through it.

Get TestFlight →
2

Tap the Jefe invite

Open this button on your iPhone and tap Accept. It opens right inside TestFlight.

Open the Jefe invite →

On a computer? Text yourself this page and open it on your iPhone.

3

Tap “Install”

In TestFlight, tap Install (or Update). Jefe lands on your home screen like any app.

Heads up: it may show up as “Sitewalk” — that's us, an earlier name.

4

Open it and walk

Open the app, try the built-in practice walk to see how it works, then do a real one. That's it — you're going.
